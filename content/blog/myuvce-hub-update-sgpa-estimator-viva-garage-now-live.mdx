Engineering exams are approaching, and we know the pressure is on. To help the 2029 Batch navigate their first year, we have officially launched two powerful utility tools on the [**MyUVCE Hub**.](https://hub.myuvce.in/login)

These tools are free, open-source, and designed specifically for the UVCE 2025 Regulations.

* * *

## 1\. SGPA Estimator (Launched Jan 21)

The new grading system is complex—with SEE marks scaled to 60 or 30 and different weightages for 2-credit vs 4-credit courses. We built a calculator that handles all the math for you.

*   ✅ **Accurate for 2025 Scheme:** Automatically handles P-Cycle & C-Cycle subjects.
*   ✅ **"Reverse" Calculator:** Enter your dream SGPA (e.g., 9.0), and it tells you exactly how many marks you need to score in the upcoming exam.
*   ✅ **Instant Results:** No login required for basic calculations.

[Open SGPA Estimator →](https://hub.myuvce.in/sgpa)

## 2\. Viva Garage (Launched Jan 17)

Don't blank out in front of the external examiner. The Viva Garage allows you to practice real questions in a gamified flashcard format. We have updated the question bank for the current semester.

**Currently Active Tracks:**

*   ⚛️ **Physics Lab**
*   🧪 **Chemistry Lab**
*   💻 **PPS (C-Programming) Lab**

Compete with your friends, track your streak, and see community stats on which questions are "Hard" or "Easy."

[Start Viva Practice →](https://hub.myuvce.in/viva)
