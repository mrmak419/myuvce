## Why This Exists

UVCE has always had knowledge—hidden in notebooks, PYQs, and canteen conversations. We just gave it a home.

> "MyUVCE.in exists to ensure student effort does not disappear with graduating batches. It is the digital bridge between the 1917 heritage and the 2026 future."
