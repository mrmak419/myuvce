# The Roadmap for [MyUVCE.in](https://www.myuvce.in)

Documenting the UVCE story through shared knowledge, student-built tools, and a culture of contribution. The Series ends, but the journey begins.

[Visit Website](https://www.myuvce.in) [Explore Tools](https://www.myuvce.in/p/student-tools.html)

## Why This Exists

UVCE has always had knowledge—hidden in notebooks, PYQs, and canteen conversations. We just gave it a home.

["MyUVCE.in](https://www.myuvce.in) exists to ensure student effort does not disappear with graduating batches. It is the digital bridge between the 1917 heritage and the 2026 future."

## What We Have Built

From the Digital Campus Map to Viva Flashcards, these tools solve real problems.

Most Popular

### Viva Flashcards

Don't panic before labs. Quick-revision flashcards designed for practical exams to help you revise concepts in minutes.

[Start Revising →](https://hub.myuvce.in/viva)

Utility

### SGPA Calculator

No more manual math. A simple, student-friendly calculator to estimate your semester performance instantly.

[Calculate Now →](https://hub.myuvce.in/sgpa)

Essential

### Campus Map

Navigate the labyrinth of the Red Building. A floor-by-floor digital guide to find labs, classrooms, and departments.

[View Map →](https://www.myuvce.in/2025/11/the-map-of-uvce.html)

Resource

### UVCE Learn

The archive of wisdom. Access organized notes, Previous Year Questions (PYQs), and lab manuals.

[Open UVCE Learn →](https://www.myuvce.in/2025/12/uvce-notes.html)

Ready to publish

### Attendance Tracker

Track your attendance transparently. Avoid last-minute shortage surprises with this upcoming utility.

[In Development →](https://hub.myuvce.in/login)

Locate

### Classroom Directory

Lost? Find exactly where your section is sitting today with the updated directory search.

[Find Room →](https://www.myuvce.in/p/classroom-directory.html)
