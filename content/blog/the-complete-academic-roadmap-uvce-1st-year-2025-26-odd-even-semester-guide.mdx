<Callout type="warning" title="Quick Status (Nov 28)">
  The honeymoon period is officially over. If you have been living on "I'll study tomorrow," well... tomorrow has arrived. 
</Callout>

Let's be real: The vibe shift from "Fresher" to "Engineering Student" hits hard in December. The chai breaks at Nandini are great, but they won't fix your attendance shortage. Make sure you are aggressively logging your classes on the **MyUVCE Hub** attendance tracker so you do not fall below the strict 75% cutoff.

**On a serious note:** The timeline below is incredibly tight. You have exactly **ONE day** of gap between your classes ending and your final exams starting. No study holidays. No breather. Just pure adrenaline and caffeine.

Here is the filtered schedule—no fluff, just the dates that will save your GPA.
