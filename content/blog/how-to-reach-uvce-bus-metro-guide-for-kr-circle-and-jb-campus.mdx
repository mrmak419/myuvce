 University Visvesvaraya College of Engineering (UVCE) operates across two campuses: the historic **KR Circle Campus** (near Vidhana Soudha) and the vast **Jnanabharathi (JB) Campus** (off Mysore Road).

Depending on your branch and year, you might need to travel to either (or both). Getting to the wrong campus on exam day is a nightmare you want to avoid! Here is your comprehensive travel guide.

## 1\. Reaching the KR Circle Campus

**Who goes here?**

*   **Branches:** CSE, ISE, ECE, EEE, and Mechanical Engineering.
    
*   **First Years:** Most 1st-year classes for these branches are held here.
    

### By Metro (The Fastest Way)

Bangalore traffic is unpredictable, but the Metro is reliable.

*   **Line:** **Purple Line**.
    
*   **Station:** **Sir M. Visvesvaraya Station (Central College).**
    
*   **Exit:** Take the exit marked for "Central College" or "UVCE". You will step out directly in front of the college gate.
    

### By Bus (BMTC)

*   **Bus Stop:** **K.R. Circle** or **Maharani College**.
    
*   **From Majestic (Kempegowda Bus Station):** It is a 15-minute walk or a short auto ride (approx ₹40-50). If you want to take a bus from Majestic, look for platform numbers heading towards Corporation/Vidhana Soudha.
    
*   **Common Bus Numbers:** Any bus in the **215 series, 300 series**, or buses going towards Shivajinagar/Majestic usually pass through KR Circle.
    

  

### Note for Mechanical Students

If you are looking for the **Mechanical Block**, it is **not** inside the main quadrangle building. It is located **across the road**. You may need to cross the street after exiting the main gate.

## 2\. Reaching the Jnanabharathi (JB) Campus

**Who goes here?**

*   **Branches:** Civil Engineering and Architecture.
    
*   **Hostels:** The main boys' and girls' hostels are located here.
    

### By Metro

*   **Line:** **Purple Line**.
    
*   **Station:** **Jnanabharathi Station** or **Pattanagere Station**.
    
*   **Last Mile:** The campus is huge (it's a forest!). From the metro station, you cannot walk to the department. You must take a **Shared Auto** or the **Campus Internal Bus** to reach the Civil/Architecture block.
    

### By Bus

*   **Destination:** **Bangalore University Gate** (Mysore Road).
    
*   **Bus Numbers:** Look for buses going towards Kengeri, Bidadi, or RR Nagar. Common series: **220, 222, 226**.
    
*   **Internal Loop:** Once you reach the University Gate, hop on the specific "Campus Loop" buses that connect the Administrative Block, Library, and Departments inside the vast JB campus.
    

## 3\. Traveling Between Campuses

During college fests like _Impetus_ or _Milagro_, or for certain labs, you might need to travel from KR Circle to JB (or vice versa).

*   **Don't take the Bus:** The road traffic on Mysore Road is heavy.
    
*   **Take the Metro:** It is the smartest option. Take the metro from **Sir MV Station** to **Jnanabharathi Station**. It takes about 20-25 minutes and is air-conditioned comfort.
    

### Summary Checklist

*   **CSE/ISE/ECE/EEE/Mech:** Go to **KR Circle** (Sir MV Metro Station).
    
*   **Civil/Architecture:** Go to **JB Campus** (Jnanabharathi Metro Station).
