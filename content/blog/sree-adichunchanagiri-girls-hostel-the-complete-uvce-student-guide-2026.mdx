Finding a secure and disciplined hostel is a top priority for outstation students joining **UVCE Bangalore**. One of the most sought-after locations for female students is the **Sree Adichunchanagiri Girls Hostel** in Mahalakshmi Layout.

## 🏠 Room Accommodation & Facilities

The living arrangements are designed to foster community while maintaining organization. Each room at the hostel accommodates six students. To ensure comfort and utility, every student is provided with the following essentials:

*   A bunker cot, a study table, and a personal cupboard.
*   Separate racks for plates, snacks, and books to keep the space tidy.
*   Access to common study areas on each floor equipped with tables for focused learning.

### 🏢 Building Infrastructure

The hostel consists of **six floors** and features a lift facility for students. However, please note that the lift is not operated during meal times to manage traffic and energy efficiency.

## 🍱 Food Facilities & Timings

The hostel follows a strict **vegetarian-only** policy. Rice-based items are a staple in all three daily meals. Non-vegetarian food is strictly prohibited inside the premises.

## 🔒 Security & Discipline

The hostel follows strict timings for the safety of all residents. The main gate opens after **6:00 AM** and closes promptly at **7:00 PM**.

## 🚇 Commuting from Mahalakshmi Layout to UVCE

Since UVCE is located at KR Circle, students staying here typically use the Namma Metro for a fast and reliable commute.

**The Metro Route:**  
1\. Board at **Mahalakshmi Metro Station** (Green Line).  
2\. Change at **Majestic** to the Purple Line.  
3\. De-board at **Sir M. Visvesvaraya Station (Central College)**.  
4\. Walk 5 minutes to reach the UVCE gate.

## 📝 Admission Process & Fees

The admission process is not handled at the Mahalakshmi Layout branch. Students must visit the **Main Branch** in person to complete formalities.

*   **Annual Fee:** Approximately ₹38,000 (Subject to change).
*   **Documents:** All necessary academic and identity documents are required at the time of admission.
*   **Main Branch Location:** XGFV+GRC, CA 18, 1st Main Road, Vijayanagar, Stage 1, Bengaluru.
