## Food Facilities & Timings

The hostel follows a strict **vegetarian-only** policy. Rice-based items are a staple in all three daily meals. Non-vegetarian food is strictly prohibited inside the premises.

| Meal | Standard Timings | Notes |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Morning** | 6:30 AM – 7:30 AM | Breakfast |
| **Afternoon** | 1:00 PM – 2:00 PM | Lunch |
| **Night** | 7:30 PM – 8:00 PM | Extended to 9:00 PM for online classes |

## Security & Discipline

<Callout type="warning" title="Curfew Rules">
  The hostel follows strict timings for the safety of all residents. The main gate opens after **6:00 AM** and closes promptly at **7:00 PM**.
</Callout>

## Commuting from Mahalakshmi Layout to UVCE

Since UVCE is located at K.R. Circle, students staying here typically use the Namma Metro for a fast and reliable daily commute. 

**The standard Metro route:**
1. Board at **Mahalakshmi Metro Station** (Green Line).
2. Change trains at **Majestic (Nadaprabhu Kempegowda Station)** to the Purple Line.
3. De-board at **Sir M. Visvesvaraya Station (Central College)**.
4. Walk 5 minutes to reach the UVCE campus gate.

## Admission Process & Fees

The admission process is not handled at the Mahalakshmi Layout branch. Students must visit the **Main Branch** in person to complete all formalities.

* **Annual Fee:** Approximately ₹38,000 (Subject to change based on the academic year).
* **Documents Required:** All necessary academic records, UVCE admission proof, and identity documents are required at the time of admission.
* **Main Branch Location:** XGFV+GRC, CA 18, 1st Main Road, Vijayanagar, Stage 1, Bengaluru.
