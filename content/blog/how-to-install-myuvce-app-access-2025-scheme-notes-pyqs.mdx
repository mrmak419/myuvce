## Installation Guides

Choose your platform below to enable the Digital Guide on your device.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Method 1: Android App (Recommended APK)">
    This is the fastest access method for the Batch of 2029 and includes a dedicated app drawer shortcut.
    
    1. **[Download MyUVCE APK](https://drive.google.com/uc?export=download&id=1woQwEmJ4ZeZGQa76dKLjVW7m1nFjER3q)**
    2. Once downloaded, tap the file and select **Package Installer** to complete the setup.
    
    *Security Note:* If Google Play Protect blocks the installation, tap **Install anyway**. We are an independent, student-run UVCE project.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Method 2: iOS / iPhone (Safari)">
    iPhones do not support APK files, and iOS blocks automated install prompts. You must use Safari to add the app to your home screen manually.
    
    1. Open **myuvce.in** in the Safari browser.
    2. Tap the **Share Icon** (the square with an upward arrow at the bottom).
    3. Scroll down the menu and select **Add to Home Screen**.
    4. Tap **Add** in the top right corner.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Method 3: Android (Browser Setup)">
    If you prefer not to install the APK and dismissed the automated popup, you can install the Progressive Web App directly from Chrome.
    
    1. Open Chrome and tap the **Three Dots (⋮)** menu.
    2. Select **Add to Home Screen** (or "Install App").
    3. Tap **Install** (Do not select "Create Shortcut" if prompted).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
