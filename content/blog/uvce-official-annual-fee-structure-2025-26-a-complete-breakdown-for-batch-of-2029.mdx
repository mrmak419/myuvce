The University Visvesvaraya College of Engineering (UVCE) has released its official fee notification for the **2025-26 academic year (Batch of 2029)**. As UVCE has transitioned into an independent university, the fee structure is decided by the Board of Governors (BoG) and follows Government of Karnataka regulations. This document is the ultimate reference for all new and continuing students regarding their financial obligations.

Understanding the fee breakdown is crucial for planning your finances over the four years of your B.Tech or B.Arch course. This guide breaks down the consolidated totals, explains what each minor fee component covers, and provides critical clarity on fee exemptions for scholarship-eligible candidates.

<Callout type="info" title="Annual Fee Totals at a Glance">
  The base annual fees (excluding examination charges) for all B.Tech and B.Arch students (Batch of 2029) are:
  * **First Year (Semester I):** ₹49,600
  * **Second to Fourth Year (Semesters III, V, VII):** ₹48,250
</Callout>

## Detailed Annual Fee Components

The annual fee is comprised of core components (like Tuition and Lab) and numerous smaller funds that support student welfare, activities, and infrastructure maintenance. Below is the full breakdown as detailed in the official UVCE Notification *(Ref: UVCE/Admn/Fee-Noti/2025-26/164/589)*.

| Particular | First Year (₹) | II to IV Year (₹) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Tuition Fee** | 32,000 | 32,000 |
| **University Registration Fee** | 4,000 | 2,500 |
| **Lab Fee** | 3,300 | 3,450 |
| **Admission Fee** | 3,500 | 3,500 |
| **Library Fee** | 1,500 | 1,500 |
| **Study Room / Reading Fee** | 1,200 | 1,200 |
| **Sports Fee + Development Fee** | 1,700 | 1,700 |
| **Medical Fee** | 900 | 900 |
| **NSS Fee** | 250 | 250 |
| **Magazine Fee** | 300 | 300 |
| **Placement & Guidance Fee** | 50 | 50 |
| **Other Misc. Funds** (Red Cross, Women Cell, etc.) | ~900 | ~900 |
| **TOTAL ANNUAL FEE** | **49,600** | **48,250** |

## UVCE Examination Fee Structure (Per Semester)

In addition to the annual administrative fee, students are liable to pay examination fees **every semester**. This covers the logistics of grading, marks card generation, and convocation.

| Component | Fee (₹) |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Examination Fees** | 2,029 |
| **Marks Card Fee** | 199 |
| **Other Fee** | 77 |
| **TOTAL (Per Semester)** | **2,305** |

<Callout type="warning" title="Late Payment Penalties">
  Strict penalties apply for late exam fee payments (I Penal: ₹220, II Penal: ₹440). It is highly recommended to pay these immediately upon notification to avoid severe issues with Hall Ticket generation or exam eligibility.
</Callout>

## Scholarship & Fee Reimbursement Clarification

The university offers strict guidelines regarding fee exemptions for eligible categories. Students must meticulously follow the State Scholarship Portal (SSP) procedures.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="SC/ST Fee Exemption Rule">
    As per the notification, SC/ST candidates (Karnataka residents) whose parents' annual income is **≤ ₹2,50,000/-** are **not liable to pay the full ₹49,600/-** at the time of admission. 
    
    However, you **MUST** apply through the State Scholarship Portal (SSP). Any fee reimbursed by the SSP must be paid to the University immediately upon receipt. Failure to clear these dues will result in the direct withholding of your original marks cards.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Other Categories (Cat 1, 2A, 3A, 3B, Minorities)">
    Candidates in these categories may claim reimbursement for Tuition, Lab, Library, Sports, and Exam fees if:
    1. Parents' Annual Income is **≤ ₹1,00,000/-**.
    2. The student has studied in Karnataka for **7 academic years**.
    
    Eligible students must proactively apply via SSP as soon as the portal officially opens for the academic year.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Essential Contacts for Fee Matters

If you encounter discrepancies or require official clarification, reach out to the relevant administrative wings:

* **SSP Nodal Officer (UVCE):** Primary contact for all scholarship applications, document verification, and SSP status checks.
* **Finance Officer (UVCE):** Contact for direct fee payments, receipt generation, and reimbursement clearances.
* **Department Chairpersons:** Responsible for attendance verification and issuing marks cards post-clearance.
