The University Visvesvaraya College of Engineering (UVCE) has released its official fee notification for the **2025-26 academic year (Batch of 2029)**. As UVCE has transitioned into an independent university, the fee structure is decided by the Board of Governors (BoG) and follows Government of Karnataka regulations. This document is the ultimate reference for all new and continuing students regarding their financial obligations.

Understanding the fee breakdown is crucial for planning your finances over the four years of your B.Tech or B.Arch course. This guide breaks down the consolidated totals, explains what each minor fee component covers, and provides critical clarity on fee exemptions for scholarship-eligible candidates.

### ⚠️ Annual Fee Totals at a Glance

The base annual fees (excluding examination charges) for all B.Tech/B.Arch students (Batch of 2029) are:

*   **First Year (I Sem):** ₹49,600
*   **Second to Fourth Year (II-IV):** ₹48,250

### What The Minor Fees Cover

While the Tuition Fee is self-explanatory, UVCE students pay several statutory and organizational fees which are often overlooked:

*   **University Registration Fee (₹4,000 First Year):** Covers the official enrollment process with the university. It drops to ₹2,500 in subsequent years.
*   **CDF Fee (College Development Fee):** A nominal charge meant to fund small-scale infrastructural development within the college premises.
*   **Welfare Funds (KSTBF/KSSWF/NETW):** Mandatory contributions to state-level teachers' and students' welfare schemes.
*   **Library & Reading Room Fee (₹2,700 Total):** Ensures access to physical library collections, digital e-journals, and designated Study Room areas.

## Scholarship & Fee Reimbursement Clarification

### 📢 SC/ST Fee Exemption Rule

As per the notification, SC/ST candidates (Karnataka residents) whose parents' annual income is **≤ ₹2,50,000/-** are **not liable to pay the full ₹49,600/-** at admission.

However, you **MUST** apply through the **State Scholarship Portal (SSP)**. Any fee reimbursed by the SSP must be paid to the University immediately upon receipt. Failure to clear fees will result in the **withholding of original marks cards**.

### Other Categories (Cat 1, 2A, 3A, 3B, Minorities)

Candidates in these categories may claim reimbursement for Tuition, Lab, Library, Sports, and Exam fees if:

1.  Parents' Annual Income is **≤ ₹1,00,000/-**.
2.  The student has studied in Karnataka for **7 academic years**.

Eligible students must apply via SSP as soon as the portal opens.

## Essential Contacts for Fee Matters

*   **SSP Nodal Officer (UVCE):** Primary contact for all scholarship applications and SSP status checks.
*   **Finance Officer (UVCE):** Contact for direct fee payments and reimbursement clearances.
*   **Department Chairpersons:** Responsible for attendance verification and issuing marks cards post-clearance.
