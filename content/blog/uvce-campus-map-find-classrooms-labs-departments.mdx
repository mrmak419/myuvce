## About This Map

Navigating UVCE can be challenging. This digital map is your solution—a **floor-by-floor guide** to the K R Circle campus, created by manually surveying every corridor.

### How to Use

**1\. Open the Menu**  
Click the 🔳 Arrow Icon at the top-left of the map title bar.

**2\. Filter by Floor**  
Uncheck boxes (e.g., "Ground Floor") to hide layers you don't need. This clears up the clutter.
